🔥 Are Wildfire Seasons Expanding?

Interactive analysis of fire season trends (2018-2025)

1. Season Length Trend (Interactive Scatter Plot)

Interaction: Click on points to highlight them. Drag area to zoom into specific years. The upward trend shows seasons getting longer.
💡 Click points to highlight • Drag to zoom • Notice upward trend line

2. Season Start & End: Slope Chart (Interactive)

Interaction: Hover over lines to highlight them. Slope direction and length directly show season expansion. Red = recent years extending longer into autumn.
💡 Hover over lines • Steeper slopes going right = longer seasons • Red lines (recent) extend further right

3. October Fire Growth: The Smoking Gun (Interactive Bar Chart)

The Evidence: October detections increased 105% from 2019 (840) to 2024 (1,728). This is the clearest proof that fire seasons are lasting longer into autumn.
💡 Hover over bars for exact values • Red trend line slopes upward = strong growth signal

4. Seasonal Intensity Profiles (Click to Toggle Eras)

Interaction: Click buttons to show/hide different time periods. Watch how the profile shape changes across eras. Recent years show flatter, wider distributions = longer seasons with persistent October activity.
💡 Click buttons to toggle • Watch October presence grow • Flatter profile = longer season
